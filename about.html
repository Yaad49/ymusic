Keybord Shortcuts

We've made navigating Y Music Page a breeze with easy keyboard shortcuts.
Press 'A' for Previous: Rewind to the previous track with a simple 'A' key press.
Press 'D' for Next: Move forward to the next track effortlessly by pressing 'D'.
Press Space for Play and Pause: Enjoy your favorite tunes with just a tap of the Spacebar. Pause and play at your convenience.
With these convenient shortcuts, exploring our music collection becomes a delightful and seamless experience. Let the melodies take you on a journey of pure musical bliss. Happy listening!
